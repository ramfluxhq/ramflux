# Maintainers

This file lists the maintainers of the ramflux core repository, as referenced
by [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md) and [CODEOWNERS](CODEOWNERS).

Ramflux is stewarded by **Span Brain**. Day-to-day maintenance and the review
routing in [CODEOWNERS](CODEOWNERS) are handled through GitHub teams under the
`ramfluxhq` organization.

## Teams

| Team | Responsibility |
|------|----------------|
| `@ramfluxhq/maintainers` | General review authority for all changes; a PR may merge with at least one maintainer approval and green CI. |
| `@ramfluxhq/security-maintainers` | Additional required review for security-, crypto-, protocol-, CI/release-, and supply-chain-sensitive paths (see [CODEOWNERS](CODEOWNERS)). |

## Maintainers

| Maintainer | Org | Scope |
|------------|-----|-------|
| Span Brain | Span Brain | Core / crypto / protocol / release |

Contact for maintenance and governance questions: **maintainers@spanbrain.org**.
Security reports must go through the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), not
to individual maintainers.

## Adding maintainers

New maintainers are added by consensus of the existing maintainers, per the
process in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md).
